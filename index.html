About Frokus

Frokus is a personal project inspired by the numerous requests I have received from my friends, seeking guidance in learning how to code. I often recommend books or courses, only to discover later that most of my friends never made an attempt or gave up midway. Frokus aims to address this issue by serving as a platform that motivates and engages these individuals, providing a valuable tool for mentors to guide their mentees effectively.

One of the most challenging aspects of learning is deciding where to start. Selecting the right course, book, or technique can be daunting and time-consuming. The sheer amount of time and effort required often leads to demotivation. A mentor can alleviate this burden by leveraging their experience to provide valuable guidance, helping learners navigate in the right direction. By investing just a few minutes with a mentor, you can potentially save hours of frustration. Frokus' mission is to enhance your entire learning process, empowering you to invite a mentor of your choice and grow together on your learning journey.

David Alberici, Creator of Frokus
